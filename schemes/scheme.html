Government Schemes

The PM-KISAN Scheme aims to provide income support to all landholding Farmers' families across the country to enable them to take care of expenses related to agriculture and allied activities as well as domestic needs

  • Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana

    Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) is the government sponsored crop insurance scheme that integrates multiple stakeholders on a single platform.

  • Weather Based Crop Insurance Scheme

    Weather based Crop Insurance Scheme (WBCIS) provides protection to the insured cultivators in the event of loss in crops yields resulting from the adverse weather incidences.

  • Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi

    Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) is the government sponsored crop insurance scheme that integrates multiple stakeholders on a single platform.

  • KCC for Animal Husbandry & Fisheries.

    To provide adequate and timely credit support under a single window to the farmers for activities related to Animal Husbandry and Fisheries

  • Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana

    Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana, Government of India is committed to accord high priority to water conservation and its management.
